# Run for ESG Senate!

ESG holds elections every semester in November and March. To prepare for election season please follow the following steps:

1. Read Article V of the [ESG Constituiton](constitution.html) and Bylaw III of the [ESG Bylaws](by-laws.html) to learn about the election procedure. 
2. Fill out this [form](http://goo.gl/forms/aHcgtoh6RP) to indicate your interest.
3. Look out for an email from our elections director!

Stay tuned for more updates in the future!
